About Us

Who

Dataflec Sdn Bhd (“Dataflec”) is a wholly owned Bumiputera company, with an aim to provide.

cost effective IT solutions to businesses, SMEs and government agencies across multiple sectors and industries.

In today’s day and age, an organisation can no longer thrive without the use of technology. Our IT solutions are designed and developed in a manner that would give your business the edge over competitors. Whatever IT challenges your organisation is facing, we can work hand-in-hand together with you in providing sustainable IT solutions to overcome these challenges..

We also strongly believe in being part of a strong IT ecosystem. To that end, we are committed to attract, motivate, develop and retain the highest quality talent available by providing our employees with a working
environment that emphasises respect, integrity, honesty and opportunity. We will serve the long-term interests of our clients and protect the security of our employees by
constantly improving and adding genuine value to all of our business activities.
